About Me

Hello! I'm Muskan Garg, a passionate and creative individual currently pursuing my BA at Gurugram University. My world revolves around the power of words – whether it's crafting compelling narratives or diving deep into the nuances of history and political science.

Beyond academics, I find immense joy in storytelling and expressing myself through various artistic mediums. I'm a budding musician, finding harmony in the melodies of the flute, and a digital artist who loves bringing imaginative concepts to life. My connection to Indian heritage deeply influences my creative pursuits.

I'm constantly seeking new challenges and knowledge, driven by my interest in preparing for UPSC exams, exploring the captivating world of animation, and discovering innovative ways to boost creative productivity. Let's connect and create something amazing together!
